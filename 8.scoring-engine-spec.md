# Scoring Engine Specification

## 1. Overview
The Scoring Engine is a backend service responsible for evaluating and ranking AI Tools and Stocks/Indices to surface the most relevant or high-performing assets to the user on the dashboard.

## 2. AI Tool Scoring
**Goal:** Determine the "Tool of the Week" and default ranking in the comparison matrix.
**Metrics:**
- **User Engagement (40%):** Number of times the tool is pinned, viewed, or added to comparisons by users.
- **Feature Completeness (30%):** Ratio of Pros to Cons and overall feature richness.
- **Market Sentiment (30%):** Extracted from recent AI News articles mentioning the tool.

## 3. Stock/Index Scoring
**Goal:** Highlight trending or highly volatile AI stocks on the homepage ticker tape.
**Metrics:**
- **Price Action (50%):** Percentage change over the last 24 hours.
- **Trading Volume (30%):** Anomaly detection in current volume versus the 30-day average.
- **News Correlation (20%):** Frequency of ticker mentions in recent scraped AI news articles.
