# Product Requirements Document

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** KordexLabs
**Objective:** To establish KordexLabs as the premier, visually immersive dashboard for the AI ecosystem, curating AI news, providing a comparison matrix for AI tools, and real-time stock tracking with a top-tier UI.

## 2. Target Audience
- Developers and tech enthusiasts discovering new AI tools.
- Investors monitoring global AI equities and indices (e.g., Nifty IT, NASDAQ AI leaders).
- Business leaders needing comparative data for software adoption.

## 3. Core Features (MVP)
**The KordexLabs Homepage:**
- Dynamic hero section with animated background.
- Glanceable widgets: Live ticker tape, top 3 trending blogs, "Tool of the Week".

**AI News (Blog Section):**
- Immersive reading view.
- Sticky table of contents.
- Estimated read time and dynamic progress bar.

**AI Tools Compare:**
- Interactive horizontal-scrolling comparison matrix.
- Color-coded tags for use cases and limitations.

**AI Stocks & Market View:**
- Tracking individual giants (NVDA, MSFT) and tech-heavy indices (Nifty 50 IT).
- Interactive, smooth line/candlestick charts showing timestamps and prices with dynamic green/red flashes.
- "Invest Now" CTA with glowing pulse effect.

**User Dashboard:**
- Personalized bento-grid layout for favorite stocks, saved blogs, and pinned tools.

## 4. UI/UX Vision
**Design Language:** Modern, data-dense but breathable. Bento-box grid layout.
**Theming:** 
- Dark Mode (Default): Obsidian backgrounds with neon accents, glassmorphism.
- Light Mode: Stark white/off-white with high contrast and subtle shadows.
**Transitions/Interactions:** 0.3-second mode toggle animations, button scaling on hover, skeleton loaders, smooth scrolling.
**Typography:** Sleek geometric sans-serif (Inter/Plus Jakarta Sans).
