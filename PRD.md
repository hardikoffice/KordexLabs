Project Name: KordexLabs
Document Version: 1.1 (Focus: Premium UI/UX & Core Features)

1. Objective
To establish KordexLabs as the premier, visually immersive dashboard for the AI ecosystem. The platform curates AI news, provides a deep-dive comparison matrix for AI tools, and delivers real-time stock tracking, all wrapped in a top-tier, interactive user interface.

2. Target Audience

Developers and tech enthusiasts discovering new AI tools.

Investors monitoring global AI equities and indices (e.g., Nifty IT, NASDAQ AI leaders).

Business leaders needing clear, comparative data for software adoption.

3. UI/UX Vision: "Creative, Amazing, & Professional"

Design Language: Modern, data-dense but breathable. We will use a Bento-box grid layout for the dashboard to organize complex information cleanly.

Theming: * Dark Mode (Default): Deep obsidian backgrounds with neon accents (cyan/purple) for an "advanced tech" feel. Uses "glassmorphism" (frosted glass effects) for cards and modals to create depth.

Light Mode: Crisp, stark white and off-white with high-contrast typography and subtle drop shadows.

Smooth, 0.3-second animated transitions when toggling modes.

Micro-Interactions: Buttons that subtly scale on hover, skeleton loaders for data fetching (instead of basic spinners), and smooth scrolling.

Typography: A sleek, geometric sans-serif font (like Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans) for high legibility on financial data and long-form blogs.

4. Core Features (MVP)

The KordexLabs Homepage:

Hero Section: A dynamic, slightly animated background (e.g., subtle glowing mesh gradient) with a clear value proposition.

Glanceable Widgets: Live ticker tape of top AI stocks, top 3 trending blogs presented in sleek image cards, and a "Tool of the Week" spotlight.

AI News (Blog Section):

Immersive reading view with a sticky table of contents, estimated read time, and dynamic progress bar at the top of the screen.

AI Tools Compare:

Interactive, horizontal-scrolling comparison matrix.

Color-coded tags for "Use Cases" and "Limitations" for quick visual scanning.

AI Stocks & Market View:

Tracking individual giants (NVDA, MSFT) and tech-heavy indices (like the Nifty 50 IT).

Interactive Charts: Beautiful, smooth line/candlestick charts that users can hover over to see exact timestamps and prices. Price changes flash green or red dynamically.

"Invest Now" CTA buttons styled with a subtle glowing pulse effect.

User Dashboard:

Personalized bento-grid layout where users can arrange their favorite stocks, saved blogs, and pinned tools.
